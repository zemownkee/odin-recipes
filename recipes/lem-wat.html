Description

If PB&J was too hard, this is for you.

  • Prep time: 5 hours
  • Cook Time: 1 min
  • Serves: 1 (Or 2 people that aren't that thirsty)

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Lemons

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Fill ice tray with water

  2. Step 2

    Put ice tray into freezer for 4 hrs and 58 minutes. Water will turn into rocks.

  3. Step 3

    Pour different water that is not in your freezer into a pitcher.

  4. Step 4

    Put water rocks into pitcher.

  5. Step 5

    Cut up lemons. Slices or wedges are acceptable.

  6. Step 6

    Put lemons in water.
