Description

This is the easiest thing. This might be easier than not cooking at all.

  • Prep time: 1 min
  • Cook Time: 1 min
  • Serves: 1 (Or 2 people that really only want a snack)

Ingredients

  • Bread - 2 slices - any kind
  • Peanut butter - 6 tbsp - smooth (unless you are a barbarian)
  • Jelly - any type but if you love your family you will choose grape
  • 1 cup beer (garnish)

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Lay down bread

  2. Step 2

    Smear peanut butter on one side of first piece of bread.

  3. Step 3

    Glob jelly onto one side of the other piece of bread.

  4. Step 4

    Refrain from putting either peanut butter or jelly on both sides of bread. This will be important for Step 6.

  5. Step 5

    Face the two sides with filling towards one another and bring them to a gentle embrace.

  6. Step 6

    Place in face.
