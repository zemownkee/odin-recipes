Description

This intermediate meal will impress your whole family. Everyone wants to eat some tacos. This is a great meal to know.

  • Prep time: 40 mins
  • Cook Time: 20 mins
  • Serves: 8 (Or 2 people that can eat more than a child)

Ingredients

Batter

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup beer
  • 1 egg

Sauce

  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup mayo
  • 1 lime
  • 1 jalepeno, minced
  • spices to taste

The Actual Taco Part

  • 1 quart oil for frying
  • 1 pound cod fillets, cut into portions
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 pack of tortillas
  • 1/2 head of purple cabbage, shredded

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Make beer batter: Combine flour, cornstarch, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl. Blend beer and egg in a separate bowl, then stir in flour mixture until combined.
